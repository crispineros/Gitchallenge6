############# Chicken Rice ###########

+Ingredients

4 Chicken breasts, cooked and small in small pieces
3 tablespoons of canola oil
1 red paprika large, chopped
1 large yellow paprika, chopped
1 large green paprika, chopped
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 Medium-sized, chopped onion
3 cups rice
5 cups of water
2 cups of vegetables mixed in pieces (carrot, peas or peas, beans or yellow corn)
1 cube of chicken broth
1 about seasoning with saffron
Salt

+Instructions
1. Place the oil in a large pot over low heat. Stir fry the three chopped peppers, garlic and onion stand up and stir until golden brown.
2. Add the 3 cups of rice, the important thing is to stir very well.
3. Add the 5 cups of water, the diced vegetables, the chicken breasts, the chicken broth cube, the seasoning sauces and the salt to taste. Stir and let cook over medium heat for about 15 minutes or until the rice is gone.
4. Once the rice has absorbed all the water, cover the pot and cook it on low heat for another 10 to 15 minutes until the rice is soft.
